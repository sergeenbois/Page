Football

Football has been a lifelong passion for me, both as a player and a fan. I actively play and follow the sport, with a special connection to the French national team and my club, Olympique Lyonnais (OL). I spend hours watching the beautiful game, analyzing tactics and enjoying the thrill of every match.

  • Favorite Teams: Olympique Lyonnais, France National Team
  • Favorite Players: Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, Neymar, Karim Benzema, Juninho, Houssem Aouar, Paul Pogba, Dybala, and many others.

Other Sports

While football is my main sport, I also enjoy volleyball and tennis. The excitement of the game, the intensity of competition, and the strategic thinking behind each play are aspects I deeply appreciate. Additionally, I love watching rugby and various other sports, especially during major events like the World Cup, the Olympics, and the Tour de France.
