Sciences: A Universe of Exploration

"The important thing is to never stop questioning." - Albert Einstein

Mathematics

From the elegance of Euler’s formulas to the infinite beauty of topology, mathematics is a cornerstone of human understanding. I’m fascinated by concepts like eigenvalues, Markov chains, and the mysteries of non-Euclidean geometry.

  • Favorite Mathematicians: Gauss, Fibonacci, Euler, Descartes, Pascal, Turing
  • Favorite Topics: Differential integrals, Chinese remainder theorem, modular arithmetic, infinity, and dimensions.

Physics

Exploring the cosmos with Einstein, diving into quantum mechanics with Bohr, or marveling at the Higgs boson, physics unveils the universe's grand design. Theoretical physics and astronomy are particularly captivating.

  • Favorite Scientists: Einstein, Hawking, Bohr, Higgs, Planck
  • Favorite Areas: Astronomy, quantum mechanics, and cosmology.

Biology

The intricacies of the human body, the marvel of evolution, and the delicate balance of ecosystems spark my curiosity. I am deeply interested in neuroscience and genetic engineering.

  • Favorite Topics: Human brain, evolution, ecology, genetics, vaccines, and pharmacology.

Computer Science

From Alan Turing’s pioneering work to today’s breakthroughs in AI and quantum computing, computer science is the language of the modern era. Cryptography and the binary foundations of computation are especially intriguing.

  • Favorite Concepts: Turing machines, quantum computing, cryptography, AI, and the principles of information.

Sociology

Understanding society through the lens of Bourdieu's "habitus," Marx's class conflict, and Weber's cultural analysis fascinates me. The interplay between sociology and economics offers profound insights into human behavior.

  • Key Figures: Bourdieu, Weber, Durkheim, Marx, Engels
  • Favorite Concepts: Cultural capital, class struggles, and societal structures.

Economics

Economics blends rational analysis with societal impact. From Keynes’ revolutionary ideas to Duflo's econometrics, it’s a domain that shapes the world. Game theory and decision-making particularly resonate with me.

  • Influential Economists: Keynes, Marx, Schumpeter, Duflo, Piketty, Tirole, Julia Cagé
  • Favorite Topics: Econometrics, macro/microeconomics, game theory, and finance.
